About me

Hi there! My name is Dahlton and I'm 23 years old, I started taking learning web development seriously around a year and a half ago during my tier 2 technical support job. I have always loved learning different languages but never found one that really clicked with me, I first learned bash for scripting when I started using Linux in early middle school. I then branched out learning little bits of multiple languages to develop and solve any problem I faced, using Arduino/C to work with embedded electronics and develop my own weather sensors, and then Python for various web scraping projects and even an Oregon Trail type game! Eventually, I found my niche in web development, I started using languages and frameworks such as HTML5, CSS3, Vanilla Javascript, and the MERN MongoDB, ExpressJS, ReactJS, NodeJS stack for full stack development. I became passionate for web development for its wide range of capabilities and widespread usability, I often would run into the issue of accessibility with showing my projects to people that may have issues installing software, from then I found with web development I would be able to continue making the things I have been and still be able to share it with anyone with much more ease. I then knew I wanted to turn my hobby into my career, I took up a bootcamp taught by Colt Steele and while learning that I made my own projects along the way and learned the importance of Github, I started trying to post daily to Github to keep my skills sharp and to continue in my pursuit of knowledge. I have a passion for learning overall and would love to pursue a life in web development. Please contact me below if you are interested in talking or working together!
